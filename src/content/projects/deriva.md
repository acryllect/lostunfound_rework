description: |
  Sofia came in with a background in classical piano and a hard drive full of field recordings from the Adriatic coast. Deriva (Drift) is the result of those two things meeting in a recording studio.

  The production is layered — voice and piano at the centre, surrounded by textures built from manipulated environmental sounds. Waves, wind, distant traffic, the creak of a wooden pier. Nothing is ornamental; every element earns its place.

  We spent a lot of time on the stereo field, giving the record a sense of physical space that rewards headphone listening in particular.
