description: |
  Titoli di Coda — "end credits" — is a record about closure. Rinaldo came in with seven songs that had been sitting in notebooks for two years, too personal to record, too insistent to ignore.

  The sessions were intense. We tracked most of the record live in the room, band playing together, with minimal separation between instruments. The bleed between mics is intentional — it gives the record a physical quality that would have been impossible to achieve by recording each instrument in isolation.

  Mixed and mastered at Lost Unfound Studio over six weeks.
