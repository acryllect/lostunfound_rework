description: |
  A four-piece band that plays like they're always about to fall apart but never quite do. Fuori Tempo is their debut EP and it sounds exactly like that — urgent, slightly chaotic, completely genuine.

  We tracked the whole band live in one room, two days straight. The arrangements were loose enough to let accidents in, and some of the best moments on the record are mistakes that got kept.

  The production sits deliberately lo-fi in texture while remaining clean in mix balance. It's meant to sound like a band you could see in a small venue — not like a polished studio product.
