description: |
  Celeste recorded Stanza Vuota over the course of a single long weekend at a farmhouse outside Forlì. The rough tracks arrived at the studio already alive — our job was to honour them rather than reshape them.

  The mix focuses on the room. You can hear the space the guitar lives in. The vocal sits forward but never feels processed. We made a deliberate decision to leave in the breath, the finger noise on the strings, the occasional creak of a chair.

  Sometimes restraint is the most difficult production choice. This was one of those records.
